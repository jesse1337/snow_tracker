Tahoe Snow Level Tracker

This website shows the historical tracking of snow levels in South Lake Tahoe (one of the most popular spots for ski resorts).

Click the button above the view details about snow levels for each month.

This graph shows the average snowfall levels in cm in South Lake Tahoe from the year 2015 onwards. The month with the highest snowfall level is December. The months of June through September have little to no snowfall.
